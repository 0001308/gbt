The History
of Compass®

Established in 1998 at Bandung, Indonesia, Compass was founded by Mr. Kahar Gunawan who wanted to rebuild a shoe factory based upon the vision left by his father, Mr. Kahar Setiadi.

The Compass ethos has always been to produce good quality footwear that reaches to a broad & diverse demography.

"It's Not A Product, It's Movement."

Gazelle
Spirit

In 2018, the Compass brand was reimagined with a fresh contemporary touch, while continuing to uphold its historical roots through the Gazelle identity.


The logo created by Mr. Kahar Setiadi in 1983, was inspired by a Gazelle, an animal known for its swiftness and agility.
